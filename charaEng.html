• SHANXI
    Shaanxi is considered to be the birthplace of Chinese shadow. Gu Jiegang, in his “the Brief History of Chinese Shadow Drama and Its Present Situation”, held that, “The birthplace of Chinese Shadow play is Shaanxi, which was the most prosperous from Qin and Han dynasties to Sui and Tang dynasties.” Xi'an is the political center of Han and Tang dynasties. Shaanxi shadow is the representative of Qin and Jin dynasties shadow styles.
    【Modeling】
    The carving style of Shaanxi shadow is mostly based on the Han and Tang character inscriptions and pottery terracotta images, with precise image design, simple facial lines, and evidently recognizable characters. There are two divisions for Shaanxi shadow: East Road, West Road. The East Road shadow puppets are larger, with upward nostril engraved, precise and rigorous in composition, simple and generous in design. In Shaanxi, the shadow females are ﬁne, digniﬁed and pure with high and protruding forehead, straight nose, long eyes, round faces, thin eyebrows, and cornered eyes, typical of “palace baby face” style in Tang Dynasty. West Road shadow puppets are exquisite, more leopard head-like with deep eyes. Shaanxi shadow has not only its own unique carving, character portrayal, but also mature stylized performance, such as eye carving, there is a differences in Phoenix eye, harsh lines, round eyes; also for the beard, it can be divided into fat beard (for frizing), leather beard, meat beard, three beard, etc. Shaanxi shadow is featured by the trichromatic colors of green, and yellow, with ink used to soften.
    【Aria】
    Shaanxi shadow has a variety of arias, such as “Laoqiang shadow,” which is popular in Tongguan, and Huayin County, Shaanxi. It is said that it has been popular since Ming Dynasty (1368-1644 AD).While singing behind the scenes, everyone in the play has his role to play and play behind the scenes, and singing with words to ﬁxed, bold, agitated tone, other personnel to echo. Only two strings, Huqin are used as the musical instruments. When singing to tension, a person will clap the board rhythmically, thus forming its singing characteristics, called “clapping shadow.” String & Board shadow aria are popular in Xianyang, Qianxian, Lixian, Liquan, Xingping, Baoji, Fengxiang and the east of Gansu. It is said that in the 18th year of Jiaqing (1831 AD) of the Qing Dynasty, it began to circulate in the folk. Singing consists of slow plate, tight board, roller board, angry board, rolling board, two & six board, and so on. Bowl-bowl aria is popular in Huaxian County, Huayin and Dali areas in the north foot of Huashan Mountain, Shaanxi Province.
  • TANGSHAN
    Tangshan Shadow is the representative of Luanzhou Shadow, with Luanzhou and Laoting shadows as the most famous. The Folk may call them as “Luanzhou Shadow”, or “Laoting Shadow.” The local shadow originated in Ming Dynasty (16th century AD). It is said that Huang Su-zhi, a scholar in Laoting County, after he failed in the imperial examination, brought back a shadow play in western Hebei to his hometown, making up and performing some “preaching & enlightenment” repertoire, which is called what we call “Laoting Shadow” now.
    【Modeling】
    The silhouette is made of donkey skin with high transparency, strong texture, tough, soft and durable, so it is also called “donkey shadow”. Luanzhou Shadow absorbs the strong points of the West Town School in its modelling, combines the artistic characteristics of paper-cut for window decoration, paper-cut and folk New Year paintings, and has a very strong folk ﬂavor and occupies a prominent position in the shadow art of China. At the same time it also occupies a very important position in our decorative art. The women images take the form of a slash from the forehead to the tip of the nose, forming a Babel nose, with upturned eyebrows and eyes, and lips slightly prominent, slightly thin chin and sharp cheeks, so that the whole face is beautiful, clear, noble, with the appearance of the traditional Chinese beauty in Ming and Qing Dynasties.
    【Aria】
    The aria of Tangshan shadow is based on Laoting dialect. Before the formal formation of vocal art, there was only pure Hem singing; till the Ming and Qing dynasties, shadow has taken the form of opera singing. In Xianfeng years of Qing dynasty, local artists invented singing with choked voice, using their hands to control the vocal cords, so that the voice is high or low. The singing is divided into ﬂat, ﬂoral, desolate, Huanyang tune and the sad tune. The Flat Tune is a singing tune of purity and chastity, ﬂoral for Dan, sad tune for a variety of characters expressing grief and sad tune is specially used by Dan.
  • HUNAN
    Changsha shadow play originated in Wanli years of the Ming Dynasty, formerly called “shadow play” or “lamp shadow play”, popular in Changsha, Xiangtan, Liuyang, Wangcheng, Ningxiang and other places.
    【Modeling】
    Hunan shadow approximates Hubei shadow in style, with the modeling perfectly round. Before Emperor Guang Xu of Qing Dynasty, the shadow was made of cattle leather, with seventy percent on the face to demonstrate the appearance and the solid background painted with color. The ﬁgure is rough and colorless. Since the end of Qing Dynasty and the beginning of the Republic of China, folk artists have used seven layers of paper papered as cardboard instead of oxen for carving, ﬁnally coloring, waxing, and embedding transparent colored paper on their clothing, that is, the making of “paper shell with positive carving, lined with colored transparent paper”, which was unique in the national shadow plays. In recent decades, the shadow face makeup have been taking celluloid as the material, shaded with color painting, and operated with only one arm.
    【Aria】
    Hunan shadow opera adopts aria from Hunan Opera and Flower Drum Opera. This is usually a three-person troupe. There are differences between the whole drama and the scattered sentences. The whole drama must be added with the stage play; the scattered sentences must be added with miscellaneous opera. Its main aria are Han tune, Flower Drum tune, Xiang opera tune, Baling tune, Hengyang tune, Siping tune, Changning tune and so on. Changning Tune is divided into big tube tune, erhu tune and horn tune.Musical instruments have drums, ideas, big gong, cymbals, small gong, trumpet, razi, erhu, big barrel, ﬁshing drum, etc. Ningxiang Shadow Opera is featured by voice-pinching.
  • YUNNAN
    Yunnan shadow is represented by Kunming small shadow and Tengchong big shadow. Kunming small shadow is invisible now; the shadow puppets popular in Yi Men, Tengchong are larger. The shadow is made of cowhide, relatively thick, and not painted in the early stages. In the beginning of the last century after the shadow reform, shadow production gradually reﬁned, portrayal and coloring skills have been developed. In particular, Liujiazhai shadow in Tengchong County, Yunnan province, is strict in leather tanning, colourful, meticulous in portrayal. Although Yunnan shadow set two arms, but mostly manipulated by one arm. In the early stage, there was head-cap separation, and there were no such phenomena now. The human modeling was clumsier, the face was round and full, and the image was plump.

Lufeng Shadow is good at folk singing and Yunnan opera and other local singing; the folk singing has its own characteristics, its singing is four sentences as a paragraph, followed by a lined word, continuous, lively and cheerful rhythm. Yimen Shadow is good at both Kushu tune (bitter vegetable tune), and Yunnan opera singing, particularly, represented by the chanting by the “swarm chore”. Yimen Shadow artist Wang Wenyue is good at storytelling and can perform with shadows for storytelling.   • LIAONING
    Lingyuan shadow is a faction of Luanzhou shadow. Legends say it was introduced from Changli, Luanxian, Leting and other places in Hebei Province, in Qing Dynasty, it has a history of more than 300 years. Xiuyan shadow was introduced in Ming and Qing dynasties, commonly known as “donkey skin shadow”, is also a popular local folk art. In addition, there are Gaizhou Shadow, Fuzhou Shadow, Jinzhou Shadow, Montenegrin Shadow, Shenyang Guanshi Shadow, Zhuanghe Shadow, Haicheng Shadow, Anshan Shadow, Kazuo Shadow, Kuandian Bahe Shadow, Dahuang Shadow and other local shadow plays, which spread widely.
    【Modeling】
    The modeling of Liaoning shadow and Luanzhou shadow is similar, with the shadow puppets made of donkey skin. The carving materials for Lingyuan shadow strictly restrict to the skin of the local donkeys with small mouth and green grooves as the best, the thickness of the skin is thin and moderate, durable but soft. The carving technique is also very particular about taking the knife and pushing the skin, taking the order of such as drilling, hole punching, carving according to the degree of complexity, with the exaggerated handling of the ﬁve parts of eyebrows, eyes, nose, mouth and mustache. The outline of the ﬁgure epitomizes in straight lines in general, with handsome image, contrasting colors.
    【Aria】
    Generally speaking, a wide range of schools of aria account for Liaoning shadow opera singing. Both Lingyuan and Kazuo shadow plays of Chaoyang area of western Liaoning, which are at the neighboring of Hebei Province, have been mostly inﬂuenced by the Luanzhou Shadow play, and the singing style similar to that of Tangshan Shadow, Laoting Shadow. Lingyuan Shadow spoken parts need to use “Laoting Tune”, more is the choking voice, tactful and delicate singing with high mellow voice.The shadow operas in other areas are directly inﬂuenced by Errentuo, Northeast Drum, folk songs, especially dialect intonation. Speciﬁcally, Laohe, Benghe, Heishui, Sanchuan and other places in Jianping County are located in western Liaoning Province; the shadow singing style is rougher with regional characteristics of western Liaoning. The shadow of southern Liaoning has the division of the south school and the north school, each has its own merits. There are also differences in the same area: Anshan Shadow Drama, for example, has formed a strong vernacular chamber music. While about 90% of the Xiuyan population of southern Liaoning is the Manchu nationality, Xiuyan Shadow opera and Manchu culture are constantly merged, gradually absorbing the local Manchu single-drum, Northeast Drum, the song-and-dance duet, Northeast yangko and other tunes, formed a strong local style of singing and shadow words; Gaizhou Shadow takes use of local colloquialism in its creation, such as the local slang for vernacular, unique singing style, thus forming its own unique tune, its music & singing is mainly divided into three kinds as shadow tune, external tune, miscellaneous tune.
  • ZHEJIANG
    Shadow was introduced by Bianjing (Kaifeng) in Southern Song Dynasty, then rooted in Lin'an, Haining, Jiaxing, Tongxiang, and developed with local folk art and customs. It has a history of more than 800 years. Zhejiang shadow refers mainly to the shadow play in Hangzhou and Haining. There are shadow puppet performances in Haining now.
    【Modeling】
    Haining Shadow scraped the sheep skin to crystal transparency, then painted with various colors, durable and beautiful. The modeling techniques include ﬂat painting use single lines, heavy color painting, and solid carving. the shadow puppets are extended from the length of the traditional length to two-foot surplus and the controlling ﬁber rods on the back side is expanded from two rods to ﬁve, It improves the space of puppets' activities, and sets up organs in the joint parts of mouth, eyes, ears and so on, so that the performance effect is lifelike and vivid. The most distinctive feature of Haining shadow is the adoptation of the drama facial makeup by using the typology principle to shape the character's loyalty, traitors, beauty and ugliness. The Southern Song Dynasty's book Dream Records said: “the faithful are carved with the right appearance, the evil carved with the ugly form; thus implying praise and derogation in it.”
    【Aria】
    Haining shadow's aria is old. At the beginning of the Southern Song Dynasty, when the shadow play ﬁrst spread into Haining, the singing was relatively simple, and later inﬂuenced by Kun and Jiangnan silk bamboo, it formed a unique tune of Haining shadow play. There are different singings between the literary drama and the martial opera, the martial opera and opening play start with Chinese opera melodies other than Kunshan and Yiyang melodies, loud and sonorous mainly with Banhu, erhu accompaniment, its main aria is Three-ﬁve-seven, Wen Erfan, Wu Erfan, Wen Sanfan, Wu Sanfan, Huilong, Jiaowanglong, etc; the literary drama is high-pitched, beautiful in tune, usually used for lyrical narration of the original drama, accompanied by ﬂute and erhu, its acoustic tunes are Long Tune, the Rising Sun over the Mulberry, Deep Worshipping, Going Upstairs, Eighteen Boards, the Head of the Court, and so on
  • HUBEI
    Hubei shadow is divided into three categories: Wei Style, Door-god Style and Hankou shadow. The shadow of Wei Style is distributed in the north and northwest of Hubei Province. It is said that in Ming Dynasty, there were shadow theatrical troupes in Fengba Township and Zhongfeng Town, Zhuxi County. The shadow of Door-god Style is mainly distributed in Jianghan Plain. It is said that the ﬁshing drum artists Pisijin and Pisiyin introduced from Sichuan during the Jiaqing period in Qing Dynasty. Hankou Shadow is popular in Hankou, Yunmeng, Huanggang and Huangshi, which began in the middle of Qing Dynasty. Among them, Yunmeng shadow has the tradition of “two performers for one play”: with the front desk singing and manipulation, the background percussion & accompaniment, commonly known as Gong or double stage, which has won the fame of light rider in shadow.
    【Modeling】
    The shadow of Wei Style belongs to the Shaanxi style, and its puppets are about one foot tall, the carving is ﬁner than the “Door-god” style, with smooth cutting, shining colors.
    The puppets of Door-god are larger, about 70cm high, carved with cowhide. Early shadow maker ground paper into paper pulp for carving, with cowhide, shadow puppet is relatively thick. The early performers carved in paper made from paper pulp, then made of cowhide, with a relatively thick silhouette. The facial makeup borrows from that of other resources, such as Peking Opera; the hand is ﬁve-ﬁngered; the face is round in outline and thick in lines. The female takes the curved moon-like eyebrows, the male straight eyebrows. Puppet clothing is a ﬂat pattern that does not care much about the actual effect, but has a certain distribution of symmetrical patterns. The crowns are diversiﬁed, and the real mustaches should be used. It is a more ancient tradition with the simple carving, realistic facial makeup.
    Hankou Shadow is about one and a half feet tall, carved with cowhide and separated heads and hats.
    【Aria】
    “Wei style” shadow sings with Zhushan dialect and Zhushan high-pitched voice, and folk percussion music, orchestral accompaniment, permeated with shaanxi opera aroma.
    Combining with the local bobbin opera (ﬁshing drum opera), Door-god style formed its own distinctive artistic characteristics. Many of the songs are based on long stories, novels, and stories of treasure scroll. The artists improvise raps according to the original story. This singing method is called “Haoshui”, while two and three ﬁshing drum artists sit and sing, which is called “meditation singing.” Mianyang Fishing Drum has ﬁve major music arias, namely: Flat opera, Sad opera, Fishtail opera (also known as Fengwei opera), Pipa opera, Miscellaneous Flower opera; with the artist Gong Benhuai’s development and creation, there are now more than 20 kinds of tunes, such as Xinping opera, Female ﬂat opera, several plates, and so on. Fishing drum takes singing mainly, saying as the auxiliary; for saying, it has scattered saying, and rhymed saying.
    “Hankou Shadow” Singing is a West Township high tune, with true and false voice, clear rap, bold tune, high-pitched like Yunmeng Wheat Horn, soft like children's cradle songs.
  • GANSU
    Longdong shadow developed early. Local legends say that in Northern Song Dynasty, Fan Zhongyan garrisoned Qingyang, local artists treated the soldiers with shadow. At the end of Qing Dynasty, General Dong Fuxiang took the shadow play from Huanxian County, Gansu Province, to Beijing to celebrate Cixi's birthday.
    【Modeling】
    Longdong shadow excels at the use of exaggerated deformation, the puppets take big head and small body, the ﬁgures are wide in its upper part and narrow in its lower part, with arms reaching over the knee. Facial images are generally the side, except some individual clowns, ghosts, which are 3/4 of the half side. The designs of facial makeup are basically the same as Qin Opera, in Guanzhong Area, Shaanxi Province, with black representing the loyal, white representing the traitors, red the brave, empty (that is, positive carving) the just. Among them, the painted face is the whole face dominated by one main color, with red, green, black, yellow and pink to match. Painted face, or the broken face, that is the face carved according to the structure of the face, or based on a certain animal plant or natural pattern, with one dominant color, other colors as the auxiliary. The outline of the image is concise and straight, the internal carving is intricate, precise, and smooth; and attention is paid to the decorative effect of the pattern; the carving colors are strong in contrast, lively and bright; the combination between the parts of the body of the shadow puppets is decomposed and reasonable, so the performance is very ﬂexible, full of embodiment of the coarse and the ﬁne, the bold and the elegant artistry.
    【Aria】
    The Taoism shadow in Huanxian County, Gansu Province is also called “Yugu Daoqing”, accompanied by ﬁshing drums and simple boards, basically a word singing based on Longdong dialect, which sounds like saying and singing.
